Institution: Water Refiling Station

This shows a database system for a Water Refilling Station. It manages employees, customers, sales transactions, containers, and container history. Sales are handled by employees and linked to customers, while containers are tracked for each transaction. The system also records container status changes over time. Overall, it helps organize data, track operations, and improve management efficiency.
